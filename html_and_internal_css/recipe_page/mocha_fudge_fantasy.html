☕🍫 Mocha Fudge Fantasy Cookies

Bold coffee meets rich chocolate in this dreamy delight!

HOME RECIPE INDEX COOKBOOK ABOUT

These cookies combine the rich bitterness of espresso with the decadent sweetness of chocolate — a treat for coffee and cocoa lovers alike.

🧂 Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp instant espresso powder (or strong instant coffee)
  • 1½ cups dark chocolate chunks

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a larger bowl, beat butter and sugars until creamy and smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, then mix in vanilla and espresso powder.
  5. Gradually blend in dry ingredients.
  6. Stir in chocolate chunks until evenly distributed.
  7. Drop rounded spoonfuls onto baking sheets.
  8. Bake 10–12 minutes until edges are set and centers are soft.
  9. Cool & experience mocha fudge fantasy in every bite! ☕🍫
