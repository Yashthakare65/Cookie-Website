✨ Cinnamon Sugar Spark Cookies

Sweet, spiced, and sparkling with flavor!

HOME RECIPE INDEX COOKBOOK ABOUT

These soft, buttery cookies are coated in a cinnamon-sugar blend that gives each bite a warm, sparkling crunch. Perfect for cozy moments!

🧂 Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp cream of tartar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 tbsp sugar (for coating)
  • 1½ tsp ground cinnamon (for coating)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, salt, and cream of tartar.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in egg and vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients.
  6. In a small bowl, mix cinnamon and sugar for coating.
  7. Roll dough into balls, then coat in cinnamon sugar mixture.
  8. Place on baking sheet and flatten slightly.
  9. Bake 8–10 minutes until edges are golden. Don’t overbake!
  10. Cool and enjoy the cinnamon sparkle magic! ✨
