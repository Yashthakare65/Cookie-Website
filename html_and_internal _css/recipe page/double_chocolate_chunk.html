🍫 Double Chocolate Chunk Cookies

Extra chocolate, extra deliciousness!

HOME RECIPE INDEX COOKBOOK ABOUT

Rich, gooey, and packed with double the chocolate! These cookies are a dream for any chocolate lover.

🧂 Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1½ cups semisweet chocolate chunks

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter and sugars until creamy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla.
  5. Gradually add the dry mixture and mix until combined.
  6. Stir in chocolate chunks.
  7. Scoop dough onto baking sheet and slightly flatten.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until edges are set.
  9. Let cool slightly and enjoy the rich, fudgy goodness! 🍫
